Why Sylven

We built this
because the
system didn't.

The Canadian public health system does extraordinary work under extraordinary pressure. It was not, however, designed for the pace at which medicine is now capable of helping people — or for the millions of Canadians managing chronic conditions, hormonal changes, and metabolic health that require more than a ten-minute appointment and a referral to a specialist with an eighteen-month waitlist.

When access to care is the barrier, outcomes suffer for reasons that have nothing to do with the quality of medicine available. Sylven exists to close that gap — by connecting patients with independent Canadian physicians who have the time to assess them properly, and with licensed pharmacies that can reach them wherever they live in Canada.

Every prescription on this platform is issued by a physician acting independently, following an individual clinical assessment. Every medication is dispensed by a Health Canada registered pharmacy. Nothing less is acceptable.
