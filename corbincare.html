About the company

A platform built for

Corbin Care Health Inc. is a Canadian health technology company incorporated in Ontario. We build and operate coordination infrastructure that connects patients with independent licensed physicians and Health Canada registered pharmacies.

We are not a clinic. We do not provide medical care directly. We build the systems that make it possible for qualified physicians to assess and treat patients efficiently, at scale, outside the constraints of the traditional appointment model.

Our patient platform is Sylven. Sylven serves Canadians seeking physician-guided programs for weight management, men's health, and women's health. You can find it at sylven.ca.

Working with us

01

Independent Physicians

We work with licensed Canadian physicians who want to extend their practice through asynchronous virtual assessment. Physicians set their own review capacity and are paid per assessment completed.

  • License in one or more Canadian provinces
  • Asynchronous model — review on your schedule
  • Per-assessment compensation
  • Full clinical independence — all decisions are yours
  • Platform and support provided
02

Pharmacy Partners

We partner with Health Canada registered pharmacies to dispense and ship prescriptions issued through the Sylven platform. We are looking for partners with nationwide dispensing capability and a commitment to discreet, reliable fulfilment.

  • Health Canada registered dispensing operation
  • Nationwide shipping capability
  • Integration with our prescription coordination system
  • Competitive volume arrangements
03

Institutional Partners

We are open to conversations with health networks, benefits administrators, and employers interested in offering physician-guided programs as a supplementary benefit to their members or employees.

  • Group access arrangements
  • White-label program coordination
  • Health spending account integration
  • Reporting and outcomes data
