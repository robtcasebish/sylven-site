1. Physician Non-Approval Refund

If the independent physician reviewing your assessment determines that the requested treatment is not clinically appropriate for you, your program membership fee for that billing period will be refunded in full.

This refund is automatic. You do not need to request it. It will be issued to the payment method on file within 5 to 10 business days of the physician's decision being recorded.

This refund policy applies regardless of the reason for non-approval, whether the physician determines you do not meet eligibility criteria, have a contraindication, or for any other clinical reason.

A non-approval refund covers the program membership fee only. It does not cover any medication costs, as medication is not dispensed unless treatment is approved.

2. Voluntary Cancellation

You may cancel your program membership at any time with no cancellation fee and no required notice period. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Program membership fees for billing periods that have already commenced are non-refundable upon voluntary cancellation. For example, if you are billed on the 1st of the month and cancel on the 15th, you will continue to have access to the platform until the end of that billing period but will not receive a refund for the remainder of that period.

To cancel your membership:

  • Log in to your patient portal and navigate to Account Settings
  • Message your care team directly through the patient portal
  • Email support@corbincare.com from your registered email address

Cancellation confirmations are sent by email within one business day.

3. Medication

Prescription medication dispensed by our pharmacy partner is non-refundable once dispensed. This is consistent with standard pharmacy practice across Canada and applicable provincial pharmacy regulations, which prohibit the acceptance of returns of dispensed prescription medications for safety reasons.

If your medication arrives damaged, is incorrectly dispensed (wrong medication, wrong dose, wrong quantity), or is subject to a manufacturer recall, please contact our pharmacy partner directly. Contact details are included on your pharmacy receipt and in your patient portal.

If your medication is lost or significantly delayed in transit, please contact our care team and we will coordinate with the pharmacy partner to resolve the issue.

4. Platform or Service Issues

If a technical failure of the Sylven platform prevents you from accessing services you are entitled to — such as a prolonged inability to access your patient portal or communicate with your care team — please contact us at support@corbincare.com. We will assess the situation and determine an appropriate remedy, which may include a pro-rated credit or partial refund at our discretion.

5. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback with your card issuer. Most billing issues can be resolved quickly and directly. Chargebacks initiated without first contacting us may result in suspension of your account while the dispute is under review.

6. How to Request a Refund

For any refund request not covered by the automatic physician non-approval refund:

  • Email support@corbincare.com from your registered email address
  • Include your full name, the email address associated with your account, and a brief description of your refund request

We respond to all refund inquiries within two business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days and will appear on your statement within one to two billing cycles depending on your card issuer.
