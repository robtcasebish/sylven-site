Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Corbin Care Health Inc.

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We collect and handle health information with the highest degree of care.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Corbin Care Health Inc. ("Sylven", "we", "us", "our"), the operator of the Sylven health coordination platform accessible at sylven.ca. We are incorporated under the laws of Ontario, Canada.

For privacy inquiries, contact us at: privacy@corbincare.com

2. Applicable Law

We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial health privacy legislation, including the Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA) in Ontario, the Health Information Act (HIA) in Alberta, and the Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) in British Columbia. In Quebec, we comply with the Act Respecting the Protection of Personal Information in the Private Sector (Law 25).

Your independent physician and dispensing pharmacist are also bound by their own professional privacy obligations under their respective provincial regulatory frameworks.

3. Information We Collect

Account and contact information: Your name, email address, phone number, province of residence, and date of birth or age.

Health information: Information you provide in your clinical intake assessment, including height, weight, medical history, current medications, symptoms, and responses to screening questions. This is sensitive personal health information and is handled accordingly.

Payment information: Payment card details are processed directly by our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full payment card numbers. We retain records of billing amounts, dates, and transaction identifiers.

Platform usage information: Log data, device information, and usage patterns on the platform, used to maintain and improve the service. We do not sell this data or use it for advertising.

Communications: Messages sent through the patient portal to your care team or physician.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate your clinical assessment and connect you with an independent physician licensed in your province
  • To transmit your health information to the independent physician reviewing your case
  • To coordinate prescription transmission to our pharmacy partner if treatment is approved
  • To process and manage your program membership and billing
  • To provide patient portal access, care team messaging, and program support
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
  • To communicate with you about your program, including physician decisions and prescription status

We do not use your health information for marketing, advertising, research without consent, or any purpose unrelated to your care. We do not sell your personal information under any circumstances.

5. Sharing Your Information

Independent physicians: Your complete health assessment and contact information is shared with the independent physician assigned to review your case. This sharing is necessary to facilitate the physician-patient relationship and is the primary purpose of information collection.

Pharmacy partner: If your physician approves treatment, your prescription information, name, and delivery address are shared with our Health Canada registered pharmacy partner for dispensing and shipping. The pharmacy is bound by its own professional privacy obligations.

Payment processor: Payment information is processed by Stripe, Inc. through their secure payment infrastructure. Stripe's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We share only the minimum information required to process your subscription.

Service providers: We use a limited number of third-party service providers for platform infrastructure, email delivery, and analytics. All service providers are required to handle your information in accordance with this policy and applicable law, and are prohibited from using your information for their own purposes.

Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or a regulatory authority. We will notify you of such requests where legally permitted to do so.

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any other third parties.

6. Health Information

Health information is among the most sensitive categories of personal information. We apply additional safeguards to health information, including:

  • Encryption of health data at rest and in transit
  • Access controls limiting who within Corbin Care Health Inc. can view health information
  • Health information is shared only with your treating physician and dispensing pharmacist
  • Health information is not used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose outside direct care coordination
  • Data is stored on Canadian servers

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal and health information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter, consistent with applicable legal and professional record-keeping requirements. Health records are generally retained for a minimum of ten years from the date of last service, or until you reach age 28, whichever is longer, in accordance with applicable provincial health records legislation.

You may request deletion of your account and non-health personal information at any time, subject to legal retention obligations.

8. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA and applicable provincial privacy law, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Withdrawal of consent: You may withdraw consent to certain uses of your information, subject to legal and contractual limitations
  • Complaint: You have the right to file a complaint with the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada or the applicable provincial privacy authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@corbincare.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, access controls, and secure infrastructure. No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

In the event of a privacy breach that creates a real risk of significant harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada as required under PIPEDA.

10. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to maintain your session, remember your preferences, and understand how the platform is used. We do not use advertising cookies or share analytics data with advertising networks. You may disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by posting the updated policy on the platform and, where required, by email notification. Continued use of the platform following notice of changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
